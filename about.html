My purpose and mission...

Seek to meet the needs of those who seek to enhance their companies and brands by bringing essential principles to my work:

  • Credibility: Customers need to feel confident about my work and professionalism.
  • Quality: Delivery of clients' projects depends on how well done it.
  • Collaboration: For both sides, collaboration is good for every piece of the project.
  • Creativity: A design company needs to highlight creative ideas.

I am Sabrina...

I would like to present my knowledge and experience with UX Design, specifically with UX Research. Collaborative and supportive are words connected with my professional and personal profile. I am looking to facilitate people's interaction with the media, prioritizing listening and observing human behavior. With a degree in Psychology, the way I connect with people and understand their needs helps me to be able to join with this ambiguous and technological world. Firstly, I would like to show my creations, broadly related to user research and methods, such as surveys, focus groups, observation, and usability tests. Second, designs with prototyping, low and high fidelity were created by Adobe XD and Figma.
